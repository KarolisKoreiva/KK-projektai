Berlin's central district is the governmental location of Germany. This makes it unique in Germany. Many companies, banks, lobby groups have their offices here.

Mitte is a popular home for young, creative and innovative companies. This start-up scene is playing an increasingly important role in the dynamic German business sector.

Berlin-Mitte has some unique cultural landscape with many historical sights and famous places, such as Alexanderplatz, Potsdamer Platz and the Brandenburg Gate.

Living space in Berlin-Mitte is demanded and rare. Many people are looking for central living spaces, but the number of available apartments is limited. New construction projects like this one create new, coveted living space.
